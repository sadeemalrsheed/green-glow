Oregano

الأوريغانو

Basic Information

Oregano is a fragrant perennial herb commonly used in cooking and traditional medicine. It thrives in warm, dry climates and is perfect for gardens in southern Saudi Arabia.

  • Type: Herb
  • Lifespan: Perennial (3–5 years productive)
  • Height: 30–60 cm
  • Blooming Season: Summer

Care & Benefits

Oregano is easy to care for and drought-tolerant once established. Its aromatic leaves are widely used in Mediterranean dishes.

  • Care Level: Easy
  • Benefits: Culinary herb, pest repellent, aromatic foliage
  • Tip: Regular harvesting promotes growth

Growing Conditions

Sunlight: Full Sun

Climate: Warm and Dry

Temperature Tolerance: Up to 40°C

Soil: Well-drained, Sandy or Loamy

Placement: Outdoor or Sunny Indoor

Watering: Light (allow soil to dry out)

Medicinal & Special Properties

Oregano is rich in antioxidants and has antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory properties. It's traditionally used for digestive issues and to relieve respiratory symptoms.

  • Medicinal Use: Natural antibiotic, relieves cough and cold
  • Key Compounds: Carvacrol, Thymol
  • Note: Best used fresh or dried in tea and cooking
