Basil

الحبق

Basic Information

Basil is a fragrant herb widely used in culinary dishes around the world. It grows quickly in warm climates and adds a refreshing aroma to gardens and homes.

  • Type: Herb
  • Lifespan: Annual (can be perennial in warm climates)
  • Height: 30–60 cm
  • Blooming Season: Summer

Care & Benefits

Basil is easy to grow and thrives in pots or gardens with good sun exposure. It’s loved for its culinary uses and insect-repelling properties.

  • Care Level: Easy
  • Benefits: Edible leaves, attracts pollinators, repels mosquitoes
  • Tip: Pinch flower buds to encourage leaf growth

Growing Conditions

Sunlight: Full Sun

Climate: Warm and Humid

Temperature Tolerance: Prefers 20–35°C

Soil: Rich, Moist, Well-drained

Placement: Indoor (sunny window) or Outdoor

Watering: Regular, keep soil moist

Medicinal & Special Properties

Basil has antibacterial and anti-inflammatory effects. It’s used in teas and traditional remedies to aid digestion and relieve stress.

  • Medicinal Use: Eases stomach discomfort, promotes relaxation
  • Special Compounds: Eugenol, Linalool
  • Fun Fact: Holy Basil (Tulsi) is highly valued in Ayurvedic medicine
