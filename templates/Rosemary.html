Rosemary

إكليل الجبل

Basic Information

Rosemary is a fragrant, woody herb native to the Mediterranean region. It's valued both as a culinary spice and a medicinal plant, often used in cooking and herbal remedies.

  • Type: Perennial Herb
  • Lifespan: 5–15 years
  • Height: Up to 1.5 meters
  • Blooming Season: Spring to Early Summer

Care & Benefits

Rosemary is relatively easy to grow with the right conditions. It’s highly aromatic and commonly used to flavor food, improve memory, and reduce inflammation.

  • Care Level: Easy
  • Benefits: Culinary use, stress relief, antioxidant properties
  • Uses: Cooking, teas, essential oils, natural remedies

Growing Conditions

Sunlight: Full Sun

Climate: Cool to Warm

Temperature Tolerance: 0°C to 30°C

Soil: Well-drained, slightly sandy

Placement: Outdoor or Sunny Balcony

Watering: Low to Moderate (let soil dry between waterings)

Medicinal & Special Properties

Rosemary is known for its medicinal properties, especially in improving memory, concentration, and hair health. It has anti-inflammatory and antioxidant effects.

  • Medicinal Uses: Enhances memory, aids digestion, relieves stress
  • Traditional Use: Used in oils and teas for healing and soothing effects
  • Other: Stimulates hair growth and improves circulation
