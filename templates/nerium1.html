Nerium Oleander

Discover details and care information for this plant

Basic Info

Type: Shrub

Life Span: 8–12 years

Nerium Oleander is a drought-tolerant ornamental shrub known for its vibrant flowers. It thrives in hot climates and is commonly used in landscapes across the central region of Saudi Arabia.

Care Instructions

  • Needs Full Sunlight
  • Thrives in Hot, Dry Climates
  • Prefers Sandy, Well-Drained Soil
  • Water Moderately — do not overwater
  • Note: This plant is toxic if ingested
