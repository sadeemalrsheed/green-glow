Date Palm

نخيل التمر

Basic Information

The Date Palm is one of the most iconic trees in the Middle East. It's highly valued for its sweet, nutritious fruits and its ability to thrive in arid environments.

  • Type: Tree
  • Lifespan: Over 100 years
  • Height: Up to 21 meters
  • Fruit Season: Summer to early Fall

Care & Benefits

Though it requires patience, the Date Palm is resilient and deeply rooted in culture and economy. It provides fruits, shade, and aesthetic beauty.

  • Care Level: Moderate
  • Benefits: Edible fruit, shade, cultural significance
  • Economic Value: A major agricultural crop in Saudi Arabia

Growing Conditions

Sunlight: Full Sun

Climate: Hot, Arid

Temperature Tolerance: Above 45°C

Soil: Sandy, Deep, Well-drained

Placement: Outdoor Only

Watering: Deep watering, especially when young
