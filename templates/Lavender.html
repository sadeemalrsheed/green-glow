Lavender

الخزامى

Basic Information

Lavender is a fragrant flowering plant known for its calming aroma and striking purple flowers. It is commonly grown for ornamental, medicinal, and aromatic uses.

  • Type: Perennial Herb
  • Lifespan: 5–10 years
  • Height: 30–90 cm
  • Blooming Season: Late Spring to Early Summer

Care & Benefits

Lavender is relatively easy to grow and thrives in dry conditions with lots of sun. It's widely appreciated for its soothing scent and usefulness in aromatherapy and herbal remedies.

  • Care Level: Easy to Moderate
  • Benefits: Aromatherapy, insect repellent, calming properties
  • Uses: Essential oils, dried flowers, herbal tea

Growing Conditions

Sunlight: Full Sun

Climate: Cool to Warm, Dry Air

Temperature Tolerance: -5°C to 30°C

Soil: Well-drained, Sandy or Loamy

Placement: Outdoor or Balcony

Watering: Low (Let soil dry completely between waterings)

Medicinal & Special Properties

Lavender has been used for centuries in traditional medicine. It's known for reducing stress, promoting sleep, and treating minor burns and insect bites.

  • Medicinal Use: Anxiety relief, sleep aid, mild pain relief
  • Essential Oil: Popular in aromatherapy for relaxation
  • Traditional Use: Used in herbal teas and skincare
