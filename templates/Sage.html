Sage

القصعين

Basic Information

Sage is a woody herb with silvery-green leaves and a strong aroma. It is commonly used in culinary dishes and traditional medicine, especially in the southern regions.

  • Type: Herb
  • Lifespan: Perennial (up to 5 years)
  • Height: 30–60 cm
  • Blooming Season: Late Spring to Summer

Care & Benefits

Sage is low-maintenance and thrives in dry, sunny environments. It offers multiple health benefits and is frequently used in natural remedies and herbal teas.

  • Care Level: Easy
  • Benefits: Culinary use, memory support, anti-inflammatory
  • Common Use: Cooking, teas, essential oils

Growing Conditions

Sunlight: Full Sun

Climate: Warm and dry

Temperature Tolerance: Up to 35°C

Soil: Well-drained, slightly sandy

Placement: Outdoor garden or pots

Watering: Moderate, let soil dry between watering

Medicinal & Special Properties

Sage has been historically valued for its healing properties. It supports digestion, helps with memory, and is believed to ease sore throats and inflammation.

  • Medicinal Uses: Digestive aid, sore throat remedy, memory booster
  • Traditional Use: Herbal infusions, incense in folk rituals
  • Essential Oils: Antibacterial, calming properties
